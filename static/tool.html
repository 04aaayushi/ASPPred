What is Antisepsis ?

The process of prevention of infection by inhibiting or arresting the growth and multiplication of germs is known as Antisepsis.

The antimicrobial products used for this purpose are known as Antiseptics, which are applied to living tissue or skin to reduce the possibility of infection, sepsis, or putrefaction. Certainly, the peptides which kill the gram-negative and gram-positive bacteria are known as Antiseptic Peptides.

What our Work Is?

ASPPred is a RF(Random forest) based prediction tool that classify the peptides into antisepsis and non-antisepsis peptides.

Machine learning is the process of teaching a computer system how to make accurate predictions when fed data.It helps to find the valuable underlying patterns within complex data and thus making predictions.
